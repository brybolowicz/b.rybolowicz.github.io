About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Bernard Rybołowicz, and I am a research associate at Heriot-Watt University and the Maxwell Institute of Mathematical Sciences. Under the supervision of Prof. Anastasia Doikou, my current research focuses on studying the set-theoretic Yang-Baxter equation and its connection with various algebraic structures.

I earned my PhD at Swansea University, where, under the supervision of Prof. Tomasz Brzeziński, I studied trusses and their connections to ring theory.

I am always happy to diversify my skills and learn new things.

If you like to learn more about my research or are interested in collaborating with me, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Current interest

  • Universal algebra (mainly ternary structures)
  • Affine geometry
  • Semigroup theory
  • Integrable systems
  • Category theory
